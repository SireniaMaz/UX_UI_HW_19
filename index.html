Hello! I am Sirenia Mazick

I am a User Experience Designer based in San Antonio, Tx with a background in Business Administration. I enjoy the challenge of putting myself in user’s shoes, anticipating their needs, and creating designs that enhance their overall experience!

My UX Skills

User Research

I utilize user persona development and journey mapping techniques to obtain a understanding of the overall user experience. I excel at identifying user pain points and translating them into actionable design solutions by uncovering valuable insights from interviews and surveys.

Prototyping

My prototyping process begins with a thorough understanding of user requirements that align with my user research. I am skilled in prototyping methods and tools, including digital wireframing tool Figma. I utilize low-fidelity and mid-fidelity prototypes as a means to conduct usability testing and extract valuable insights from user interactions.

Information Architecture

I apply IA best practices, such as card sorting, user flows, and hierarchical organization, to create navigation systems that allow users to effortlessly find the information they seek. In a recent project, my IA redesign efforts resulted in a significant improvement in findability and user satisfaction.

My Work

Non-Profit RWD Redesign

Redesigned Compass Connections, a local non-profit into a responsive website prototype. It’s to provide an easier way to locate fostering and adoption information for prospective foster parents.

Government Website RWD Redesign

Redesigned the National Weather Service government website into a responsive design to ensure optimal performance across different devices. I integrated new interactive features, and utilized emerging design techniques that improve the overall user experience.

Shelf Esteem Mobile App
